About AI Prompt Splitter

1. Our Mission

To empower AI users by eliminating the "Lost in the Middle" phenomenon, ensuring Large Language Models (LLMs) can process massive documents with 100% accuracy and contextual retention.

2. The Problem: "Lost in the Middle"

When feeding extensive texts into AI models (such as ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini), the models often forget or hallucinate information located in the middle of the prompt. Manually splitting these documents is inefficient, error-prone, and risks breaking the logical flow of your data.

3. Our Solution

AI Prompt Splitter is a precision-engineered context chunker. It intelligently divides your oversized texts into optimized, safe-sized blocks. This ensures every piece of information is perfectly digested by the AI, maintaining the integrity of your original document.

4. Privacy First: The Zero-Leak Architecture

Security is our uncompromising baseline. Built on a strict Zero-Server architecture, AI Prompt Splitter operates 100% locally within your web browser. Your text is never uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any external database. Your confidential data remains exactly that—confidential.

5. Free & Accessible

We believe high-quality productivity tools should be accessible to everyone. To keep AI Prompt Splitter entirely free for the global AI community, our platform is supported by unobtrusive Google AdSense advertisements.
