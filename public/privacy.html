Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

1. Zero-Server & Local Processing

AI Prompt Splitter is designed with absolute privacy as its core principle. The tool operates completely as a client-side application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any text you paste into the tool. All text chunking and processing happens 100% locally within your device's browser memory.

2. Data Collection & Analytics

Because our tool operates locally, we do not have a database and do not harvest user-generated content. We may use basic, anonymized web analytics to monitor overall site performance and traffic, which does not identify individual users or their personal data.

3. Google AdSense & Cookies

To keep this tool free, we use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

We respect global privacy standards. Since no personal prompt data is transmitted to or stored on our servers, you retain complete ownership and control over your input data at all times.

5. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date". Your continued use of the tool constitutes acceptance of those changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy or our Zero-Leak architecture, please contact us at: contact@promptsplit.net
