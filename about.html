Better Context, Better AI

01 The "Lost in the Middle" Problem

When feeding Large Language Models (LLMs) like GPT-4, Claude, or Gemini with massive amounts of text, they often struggle to maintain accuracy across the entire context. This is a documented phenomenon called "Lost in the Middle." AI Prompt Splitter helps you stay within the optimal "sweet spot" of AI reasoning by breaking long texts into manageable, paragraph-aware chunks.

02 Why Zero-Leak Architecture?

Security is our core foundation. We believe that professional tools should never compromise your data privacy. Our **Zero-Leak Architecture** ensures that every character you paste is processed 100% locally in your browser memory. No data is ever uploaded to a server, keeping your sensitive logs, codes, and documents completely private.
